How to Install

1

Open in Chrome (Android) or Safari (iOS)

The app works best in Chrome on Android and Safari on iPhone/iPad

2

Tap the browser menu

Android Chrome: Tap the 3-dot menu ⋮ then Add to Home screen

iPhone Safari: Tap the Share button ↑ then Add to Home Screen

3

Tap "Add" or "Install"

The app will appear on your home screen like any other app — no App Store needed!
